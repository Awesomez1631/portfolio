Clash Royale

Clash Royale

This game is about strategy. In this game there are many cards or characters with different abilities - one can defeat another one and the other one can defeat a different character. Taking all cards you have into consideration you form a deck with 8 cards. Using that 8 cards you can battle against different people. There are 3 towers. If you destroy the king tower it means you have won. If not, the player who has destroyed most towers wins. There are differnet modes such as 2v2 or 1v1. There are many tournaments too. And, You can even form a group and talk or play battles with each other.

Bloons Td Battle

Bloons Td Battle

This game is about strategy. In this game there are many characters and arenas. You can get different characters buy either buying them with game coins or unlocking them. These characters you can upgrade. There are many modes and tournaments. When you start a battle, you have to chose 4 cards which you think are best suitable for the arena. Then when you enter a battle, you have to blast the balloons and when the levels increase the strength of the ballon increases. When you blast a balloon, you earn money. With this money you can upgrade your cards or send your opponents stronger and more cards. You have 100 health points. Whoever reaches 0 health points first loses.
